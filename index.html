Nagar pustakalaya Deoghar Is A Public Library Established On 1996 By late Cheif Minister of Bihar Pandit Govidanand Jha.
The Library started to taking its shape after coming Sri manoj Kumar Mishrain 2006.He is the person who dedicated their whole life for library. The ground floor of library is constructed by Fund provided by Nagarpalika Deoghar.1st and 2nd Floors are constructed by Ex. M.P. Sri Pradip yadav.

Facilities

The Library Provide A good Seat,Table,Light,Fan,Water,Toilet Room,Cycle stand and a colllection of approx.14000 Books of all category.
The fees is only 120 Rupees per month.
